Vacation Planning Checklist
===========================

Before Trip:
□ Book flight tickets
□ Reserve hotel accommodation
□ Apply for visa (if needed)
□ Pack suitcases
□ Arrange pet care
□ Hold mail delivery
□ Backup important documents

During Trip:
□ Check-in to hotel
□ Visit local attractions
□ Try local cuisine
□ Take lots of photos
□ Buy souvenirs

After Trip:
□ Unpack luggage
□ Process photos
□ Write trip journal
□ Share experiences with friends
